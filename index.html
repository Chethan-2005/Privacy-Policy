Privacy Policy - RenalGuard

Last updated: October 2025

Welcome to RenalGuard – IJV Line Risk and Dialysis Health Management. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our app. By using RenalGuard, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

To provide our medical support services, we collect:

  • Name, email, and contact number (for both doctors and patients)
  • Doctor profile details (specialization, education, location)
  • Patient medical data including age, gender, and health parameters

1.2 Health and Assessment Data

RenalGuard records assessment scores and risk-related data to calculate IJV line risk and dialysis-related outcomes. This includes line duration, comorbidities, and other clinical metrics.

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and error logs (for performance improvement)
  • Approximate location (only when required for clinical use)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage doctor–patient records securely
  • To calculate and store dialysis risk assessments
  • To remind users about IJV line duration and medical follow-ups
  • To improve app features and ensure patient data accuracy
  • To contact users about account or data-related issues

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All information is securely stored in encrypted databases
  • Passwords are encrypted and never shared
  • HTTPS is used for secure data transmission
  • Access is restricted by user role (doctor, patient, admin)

4. Data Sharing Policy

RenalGuard does not sell or rent personal data. Data may be shared only with authorized medical staff or administrators for clinical management purposes. We do not disclose information to third parties except where required by law.

5. User Rights

  • Access and update your personal data anytime
  • Request correction or deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection
  • Opt out of assessment or reminder notifications

6. Children’s Privacy

RenalGuard is intended for licensed medical professionals and adult patients only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 13 years of age.

7. Data Retention

Medical and assessment data are retained only as long as required for clinical management or as mandated by law. Upon account deletion, all data will be permanently removed from our servers.

8. Third-Party Integrations

  • Firebase (for authentication and secure data storage)
  • Retrofit API for backend communication
  • Google Play Services for app stability

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. All updates will be reflected within the app and on our official website. Continued use of RenalGuard after changes indicates acceptance of the revised terms.
